How to Play

To play, there are two ways:

You can either click on the card itself to flip it and confirm your educated guess or you can drag the food item down to the panda and see how it turns out for her when you do! Have fun and help Panda get better!

Carrot

They are good for you! Carrots have vitamin A and C in them that helps boost immunity, helping you with fighting off cold symptoms and preventing new ones.

Banana

They are good for you! Bananas are rich in potassium that helps fend off bloat and magnesium that helps fight pain, easing the lack of appetite you feel when sick.

Eggplant

They are actually not great to eat when sick! They contain high levels of histamines that will make your nasal passage swell and make your congestion worse.

Rice

This can be good for you! Rice contains tryptophan, an amino acid that can help induce sleepiness. Congee is a way to go!

Strawberries

They are surprisingly bad for you during a cold! They release histamine that can contribute to congestion. Stay away from them until you get better!

Avocado

Not a good idea! This fatty fruit is high on histamines - eating more of it will make your runny nose worse! Keep the guac for when you are feeling better!

Coffee

Save the morning joe for brighter days! Coffee will only dehydrate your body when it needs water the most. That latte will have to wait.

Pineapple

Great for that stuffy nose! Pinneapple contains bromelain that has been shown to thin mucus. Time to chop down on a few!

Water

Drink plenty of fluids! Being hydrated is your quickest way to recovery!

Junk Food

Avoid avoid avoid! Eating junk food can trigger headache as well as dehydrate you!

Papaya

Not today! Papaya is a fruit that contains histamines that will make your nasal passage swell. Save it for another time!

Alcohol

That sip is going to have to wait! Alcohol can take a huge toll on your immune system. Drinking too much can make you suspectible to illness 24 hours after imbibing. Better to not drink it!

Orange

Caution - do not eat! Citrus fruits actually may irritate your throat, which could make you feel worse!

Honey

Good! Having honey can not only help clear your throat but it's also rich in potassium that helps fend off bloat and magnesium that helps fight pain. Eat up!

Shrimp

Plan for another day! Shellfish like shrimp contain compounds that trigger the release of histamine, which will only make you feel more congested and stuffy.

Sweet Potato

Defintely eat! Eating sweet potatoes will help you in boosting your immunity with vitamin A and C.

Pumpkin Seeds

Yup! Pumpkin seeds contains zinc that can reduce cold symptoms and boost your immunity!

Coconut Water

Drink up! Replacing lost electrolytes like sodium, magnesium, and potassium will help speed up your recovery - something that coconut water can help with!

Ice Cream

Keep the dairy in the fridge! Dairy products like ice cream is high in inflammation-triggering saturated fat. Keep the scoops and fun for another time!

Pizza

Slices or pies - do not eat when sick! The cheese on pizza can cause inflammation due to saturated fat and tomatoes are known histamine releasers. Avoid for now!

Bacon

Raincheck! Bacon contains high sodium that can lead to dehydration when ill.

Candy

Nada - sugar causes inflammation and will decrease immunity. Savor the sweetness another time!

Tuna

Seafood is great - just not when you're sick! Tuna is particularly high in histamines, which can increase inflammation and contribute to congestion.

Hot Chili Pepper

Sure - in moderation! Hot chili peppers has a mucokinetic effect that can make mucus thinner and you less congested. Just don't eat too many!
